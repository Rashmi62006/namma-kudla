Delicacies of Mangalore

Discover something unique and delicious from Mangalore

Neer Dosa

Neer dosa, which translates to "water dosa," is a thin, lacy rice crepe originating from the Tulu Nadu region of Karnataka. Quick, light, and delicious.

Mangalore Buns

Soft, fluffy, and slightly sweet deep-fried bread from Udupi-Mangalore. Made with all-purpose flour, ripe bananas, sugar, and yogurt.

Sea Food

Fresh, flavorful seafood dishes like Anjal Tawa Fry, Crab Masala, and Prawns Ghee Roast, served with rice or rotti and regional spices.
