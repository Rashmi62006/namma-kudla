Destinations in Mangalore

Someshwara Beach

Mangalore is famous for its fresh, flavorful seafood, which is integral to its cuisine and community. Popular dishes include Anjal Tawa Fry (Seer Fish), Crab Masala, and Prawns Ghee Roast, often featuring regional spices and served with rice or rotti.

St. Aloysius Chapel

St. Aloysius Chapel is situated in the heart of Mangalore city on the Lighthouse Hill. Built by Jesuit Missionaries in 1880, its interiors were painted by Italian Jesuit Antonio Moscheni in 1899.

Kudroli Temple

The Kudroli Gokarnanatha Temple is a Hindu temple in Mangalore, Karnataka, dedicated to Lord Shiva (Gokarnanatha).
