Mangalore Experiences & Delicacies

Panambur Beach

Panambur Beach is one of the most popular beaches in Mangalore, known for its scenic beauty, camel rides, and water sports.

Pabbas

Pabbas is a famous ice cream parlour in Mangalore, known for its delicious and unique ice cream flavours loved by locals and tourists alike.

Gadayikallu Hill

GGadaikallu, also known as Jamalabad Fort, is a historical trekking destination near Belthangady, mangalore, famous for its challenging ascent up nearly 1,800 stone steps to a hilltop fort built by Tipu Sultan
